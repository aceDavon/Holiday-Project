COUTURE BRIDAL, OCCASION AND EVENING WEAR

Our London store is a haven in the heart of Kensington and offers an exclusive setting for stylish women seeking a unique and bespoke retail experience.

You will find a carefully curated edit of Sassi’s bridal designs in our west London boutique. Browse couture wedding gowns, sophisticated occasion wear and fabulous evening wear in an elegant and spacious setting. Our knowledgeable stylists love nothing more than sharing their expertise and guiding clients through the design possibilities and process of creating an iconic Sassi Holford outfit.

Ready to begin your journey with us? Our London store is now open and taking appointments in line with government guidelines.

VIRTUAL CONSULTATIONS ON ZOOM

When you book your consultation, in the ‘YOUR QUERY’ box please put your availability for the consultation plus the date and venue of your wedding. If you wish you can list the Sassi Holford dresses you like and add a link to your wedding dress Pinterest board if you have one.

We will contact you to confirm the 30 minute Zoom appointment and we are more than happy for one or two of your family or friends to join us on the call and share this experience with you.

Virtual Consultations are available with our London and Taunton Consultants.

TAUNTON

store

BRIDAL, OCCASION AND EVENING WEAR

Located in the South West of England, our Taunton boutique is an exclusive retreat for brides searching for their dream wedding dress. The elegant and intimate environment is the perfect setting for your once-in-a-lifetime shopping experience.

We’re passionate about turning your dreams into reality and we pride ourselves on our expertise and the personal consultations we offer. During your exclusive appointment our time is dedicated entirely to you and your wedding dress journey. The knowledgeable and friendly staff are experts at guiding brides through all their styling decisions. As well as Sassi Holford’s exclusive bridal collection, in our Taunton store you will also find carefully selected wedding dress collections from invited designers including Romantica of Devon and Maggie Sottero.

Our Taunton store is now open following government guidelines. To take the first step towards finding your dream dress, call our friendly team today to book your personal consultation.
